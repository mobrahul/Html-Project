Description

A warm and healthy breakfast made from oats, milk, and toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups milk or water
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Fresh fruits (e.g., bananas, berries)

Directions

  1. Bring milk or water to a boil.
  2. Stir in oats and reduce heat.
  3. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add honey and fruits on top.

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 150
  • Protein: 5g
  • Carbs: 27g
  • Fiber: 4g
  • Fat: 3g
