Description

Fluffy pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Whisk milk and egg together.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Cook on a skillet until golden brown.

  • Carbs: 30g
