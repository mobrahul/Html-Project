Fruit and Yogurt Parfait

Description

A refreshing and healthy snack made with layers of yogurt, fruits, and granola.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Directions

  1. In a cup or bowl, add a layer of yogurt.
  2. Add a layer of granola and a layer of berries.
  3. Repeat the layers until the cup is full.
  4. Drizzle honey on top and serve.

Peanut Butter and Banana Toast

Description

A quick and satisfying snack with peanut butter, banana slices, and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice whole-grain bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • A pinch of cinnamon

Directions

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
  2. Spread peanut butter on the toast.
  3. Place banana slices on top.
  4. Sprinkle with cinnamon and serve.
